Hello, and welcome to our website! Here you can find out more about our services, our house and the island it is located on. Location

Our house is situated in between two small towns: Murter and Betina, both of which can offer you coffee bars, patisseries and restaurants. In murter, during season, it is not uncommon for there to be a show during evening hours, which brings some nightlife for this othervise very peacefull place.

From our house, the distances to numerous points of interest are:

  • Market - 300m
  • Beach - 70m
  • Restaurant - 200m
  • Pharmacy - 600m
  • Bus station - 300m
  • Murter town center - 400m
  • Betina town center - 800m
Other beaches If by any chance our closest beach doesn't suit your needs, don't worry: There are plenty of them on the island The most popular one is by far Slanica. It is a sand beach with a smooth drop and great waves. Alongside it there are two coffee bars, a restaurant, a few fast food vendors and a large water toboggan ride! The second most relevant beach is Kosirina. It is a pebble beach, and features two coffee bars alongside it.
